Retail Warehouser

Greet customers and load warehouse products, yard products, and eComm merchandise. Provide product information and keep warehouse and yard areas clean and orderly. Unload, check-in, and stock warehouse and yard area merchandise.

Including but not limited to the following:

  • Provide customer service and roduct information to customers on warehouse and yard products.
  • Safely and carefully carry out load and transport merchandise using established standards, policies and processes.
  • Ensure a safe working environment by wearing appropriate safety garments and following all safety procedures.
  • Execute eComm activities as required by BOPIS.
  • Keep store warehouse and outside facility areas clean and orderly using our daily warehouse and yard recovery process.
  • Unload, check-in, and stock warehouse and yard merchandise utilizing established procedures.
  • Responsible for properly securing warehouse, yard, and lot merchandise at Wilco Stores and while using equipment/vehicles.
  • Keep the store stocked with merchandise warehoused or stored in the yard or warehouse.
  • Safely operate forklift and delivery vehicles.
